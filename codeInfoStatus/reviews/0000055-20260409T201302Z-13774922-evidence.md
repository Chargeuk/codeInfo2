### Description

- Story 55 adds one durable Mongo-backed queue for ingest and re-embed requests, keeps runtime execution single-flight, preserves blocking behavior for re-embed callers while queued work waits, and exposes queue identity through `requestId` plus later `runId`.
- The story also requires queued visibility in the shared repository-list contract and the ingest UI, startup recovery that prefers `cleanup-blocked` then leftover `running` work before newer `waiting` work, duplicate collapse by canonical target path, and explicit `QUEUE_UNAVAILABLE` transport behavior when Mongo-backed queue persistence is unavailable.

- Multiple active ingest workers at once
- Degraded queueable execution when Mongo is unavailable
- A dedicated queued-work card outside the repository list
- Queue-position fields on unrelated non-repository read surfaces
