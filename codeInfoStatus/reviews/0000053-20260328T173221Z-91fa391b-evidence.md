## Canonical Plan Summary

### Description

- Fresh flow starts must get a new `executionId`, a new parent flow conversation, and a new per-execution child-conversation map.
- Resume must stay on the same parent conversation, same `executionId`, and same child conversations.
- Child conversations stay visible and usable in Agents.
- Repeated executions are distinguished by metadata-only `Run <shortExecutionId>` clues in the shared sidebar UI.
- The story explicitly avoids new services, new listeners, a title rewrite, or a broader execution-history feature.

- Execution-scoped server persistence and runtime reuse boundaries.

- Final wrapper, e2e, and manual proof of the completed behavior.
