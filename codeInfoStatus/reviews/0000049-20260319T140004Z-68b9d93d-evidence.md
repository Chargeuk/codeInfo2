## Plan Extract

### Description

- Story 49 addresses client-side long-transcript performance, especially Agents input lag when a long transcript is visible.
- The story replaces three page-local inline transcript renderers with a shared client transcript layer for Chat, Agents, and Flows.
- The implementation is explicitly client-first, but the story allows one narrow server-side exception for stop-near-complete status alignment and stop-path diagnostics.
- The optimized transcript must preserve newest-last ordering, rich transcript features, hydration and in-flight merge behavior, Flows retained-assistant behavior, pinned-bottom versus scrolled-away behavior, and row remeasurement after streaming or expansion.
- The story is expected to use virtualization for long transcripts and to keep page-specific controls outside the shared transcript subtree.
