### Why this is a finding

That means a mixed-version or corrupted persisted batch payload can be rendered as a benign skip warning, which hides the real producer/consumer mismatch and makes transcript/debug output misleading.

This story explicitly relies on warnings to make fallback, skip, and repository-failure behavior observable. Rewriting an unknown warning code into a known benign code weakens that contract and can hide malformed persisted state, partial migrations, or future producer regressions behind a successful-looking transcript.
