## Rejected Risk Notes

### 1. `startFlowRun(...)` in `server/src/flows/service.ts`

- Candidate risk challenged:
  - A legacy stopped parent conversation missing `flags.flow.executionId` could still partially persist parent and child state out of order, or a fresh run from a selected older conversation could accidentally reuse the old conversation id.
- Decision:
  - Rejected risk.
- Evidence:
  - [server/src/flows/service.ts](/Users/danielstapleton/Documents/dev/codeinfo2/codeInfo2/server/src/flows/service.ts) persists legacy parent resume state before `validateResumeAgentConversations(...)` when `resumeStepPath` is present, and fresh run selection uses a new conversation id when an existing conversation is present without resume.
  - Direct proof exists in [server/src/test/integration/flows.run.resume.test.ts](/Users/danielstapleton/Documents/dev/codeinfo2/codeInfo2/server/src/test/integration/flows.run.resume.test.ts) for legacy parent backfill, conflicting child markers, missing child mappings, and fresh-run behavior.
- Remaining weakness:
  - Standard-path launcher reachability for every integration path is still partly indirect and depends on unchanged wrapper and route wiring.

### 2. `ensureFlowChildConversationOwnership(...)` in `server/src/flows/service.ts`

- Candidate risk challenged:
  - Resume could silently accept a missing child conversation, wrong-agent child conversation, or mismatched child execution marker by normalizing bad state into success.
- Decision:
  - Rejected risk, with one residual weak-proof concern.
- Evidence:
  - [server/src/flows/service.ts](/Users/danielstapleton/Documents/dev/codeinfo2/codeInfo2/server/src/flows/service.ts) explicitly rejects missing child conversations, agent mismatches, and conflicting child execution markers, and only backfills when the child execution marker is absent.
  - Direct proof exists in [server/src/test/integration/flows.run.resume.test.ts](/Users/danielstapleton/Documents/dev/codeinfo2/codeInfo2/server/src/test/integration/flows.run.resume.test.ts) for missing and conflicting child mappings.
- Remaining weakness:
  - The whitespace-only child execution marker variant remains weakly proven rather than directly isolated in its own test.

- Candidate risk challenged:

- Evidence:
