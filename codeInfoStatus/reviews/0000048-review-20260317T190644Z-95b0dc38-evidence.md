## Plan Summary

### Description

Story 48 combines four coordinated workstreams:

1. Switch referenced-file lookup from owner-first to working-repo-first with one shared repository-candidate contract.
2. Persist, restore, edit, validate, and clear working-folder state across chat, agent, flow, and direct-command surfaces.
3. Complete a repo-owned environment-variable cutover to `CODEINFO_*` and `VITE_CODEINFO_*`, including the later runtime/server env follow-up and the final root `.env.e2e` cleanup.
4. Replace OpenAI heuristic token counting with tokenizer-backed counting aligned to the provider’s 8192-token truth and fail-closed behavior.

The plan also requires shared lookup/debug markers, compact persisted lookup summaries, fail-fast behavior for higher-priority parse/validation/decode failures, reuse of the existing conversation-based storage model, and wrapper-first closeout evidence.
