In `server/src/chat/reingestStepLifecycle.ts:270-304`, any unknown single-result `targetMode` falls through to `'sourceId'`, which makes a corrupt persisted tool-result look like a valid selector-driven re-ingest in assistant text, persisted `toolCalls`, and lifecycle logs. This is backward-compatibility-unclear rather than intentionally breaking: accepting legacy `current` is planned, but collapsing arbitrary malformed single-result modes into canonical `sourceId` hides bad data instead of surfacing it.
