## Plan Extract

### Description

- Story `0000055` adds a durable Mongo-backed whole-request queue for ingest and re-embed work.
- Queue identity is request-oriented: `requestId` is durable queue identity, `runId` appears only after execution starts.
- Waiting duplicates should reuse and update the existing waiting queue record instead of creating a second item.
- Startup recovery must resolve `cleanup-blocked`, then abandoned `running`, then the oldest remaining `waiting` item.
- Repository-list readers are the source of truth for queued visibility in the ingest UI and related listing surfaces.

### Out Of Scope

- User-driven queued-item cancellation or removal.
- Priority or non-FIFO scheduling.
- Multiple concurrent ingest workers.
- A degraded immediate-run mode when Mongo queue persistence is unavailable.
- A separate `deduped` response field.
